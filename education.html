Racism

The IRR has produced a series of key print and multi-media publications on aspects of racism which are suitable for use in formal and informal educational settings. Some of these are available to purchase, others are produced as free downloads from this site. In addition, teachers and students are referred to the definitions , statistics section and the IRR’s Briefing Papers.

Black History

The IRR has published a number of pamphlets on black British history and a free download for use in schools. Those researching aspects of black British history in more depth may wish to consult articles published in Race & Class and the Black History Collection held at IRR.
